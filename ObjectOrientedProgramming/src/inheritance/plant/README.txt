Plant Class:
//Beside the bicycle project, Plant project is an example of inheritance.
1) Plant has type, color and leaves and also has methods needWater(), oxygenCycling(), CO2Obtaining and printInfo().
2) It has a contructor method.

Bloomer Class:
//Bloomer class inherit/extends from Plant class.
1) Bloomer has flower except like plant.
2) Also it has a method named haulingBees ().
3) In constructor method, I used super() and this() for initilization.

FruitTree Class:
//FruitTree class inherit/extends from Bloomer class.
1) It has flower.
2) In constructor method I used super () and this() for initilization.
3) Also it has a method called produceFruit().
